Helvetica

Helvetica is an neo-grotesque or realist sans-serif typeface designed by Max Miedinger in 1957. Max Miedinger was a Swiss typeface designer. The typeface was influenced by German and Swiss designs, and is said to be based off of the famous 19th century typeface Akzidenz-Grotesk.
